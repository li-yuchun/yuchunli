About

Hi, I'm Yuchun.
I'm a service designer with an interdisciplinary background. I worked in marketing for three years, and I particularly enjoyed creating creative solutions to solve my client's needs. Besides, I care about what value is being generated in the market. As a result, I decided to bring my skill sets with a customer-centric mindset, moving to a new career as a service system designer and design for various user's needs.

I love to work on user journeys in a bigger context and investigate how users' physical experience and digital experience are intertwined. Through empathizing users' needs and analyzing their patterns, I transform the insights into potential solutions.

In my previous experience, I've worked with Volvo to develop a personalized car-sharing service and B&O to discover current service offerings and the product value in the development team. I'm based in Copenhagen with the flexibility to work remotely or relocate to other European cities.
