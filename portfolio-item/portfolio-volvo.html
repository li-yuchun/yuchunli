Personalized Service For Volvo

Explore 4 service prototyping approach for a personalized car-sharing service

The emergence of car-sharing services gives the car manufacturer a new role as service provider. This leads to several new challenges such as, lack of profitable business models and different value perception about car.

Personalization is perceived as a competitive edge for digital services to elaborate the service quality. The project aims to use service prototyping as a mean to examine this assumption by developing a new car-sharing service.
